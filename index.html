Easy mode tracks score and elapsed time, with no move counting. Normal mode gives you eight minutes and a 13 move limit. Hard mode gives you five minutes with 25 grace moves, then extra moves cost points.

Goal

Rotate every pipe until clean water reaches the village.

Avoid

Contaminated pipes reduce your score and block the flow.

Bonus

Collect floating water drops for extra points.
